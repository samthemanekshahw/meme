## Humor Amplification Mechanisms

### 1. Amplification
Take subtle emotions and crank to maximum:
- Mild annoyance ‚Üí Explosive rage
- Slight tiredness ‚Üí Complete mental breakdown
- Small win ‚Üí Ultimate victory celebration

### 2. Irony/Contrast
- Serious clip under absurd situation
- Happy clip under sad situation
- Calm clip during chaos

### 3. Parallel Situations
- "Same energy" moments
- Different contexts, same vibe
- Universal experiences in specific scenarios

### 4. Cultural Layering
- Reference memes within memes
- In-group knowledge rewards
- Multiple layers of meaning

### 5. Unexpectedness
- Surprise factor
- "Holy shit that's genius" moment
- Perfect once seen, unexpected before

## Analysis Process for New Memes

1. **Identify** the core situation/emotion/absurdity
2. **Determine** emotional tone (unhinged, exhausted, triumphant, defeated, confused, angry, absurd, dark, chaotic, calm)
3. **Consider** amplification angle (aftermath, cause, perspective, extreme version, ironic contrast)
4. **Brainstorm** meme-famous clips matching this energy
5. **Craft** short caption using dank language
6. **Verify** the combination makes it significantly funnier
